IndiGo Airlines

IndiGo is India's largest passenger airline with over 50% market share. Known for punctuality, affordable fares, and efficient service, it operates across 70+ domestic and 25+ international destinations.

Air India

Air India is the national carrier of India, now owned by the Tata Group. It connects major global cities with India and is known for its international network and full-service offerings.

Vistara

Vistara is a premium Indian airline, a joint venture between Tata Sons and Singapore Airlines. Known for luxurious services and modern fleet, it operates both domestic and international flights.

Alliance Air

Alliance Air operates regional routes within India, often connecting tier-2 and tier-3 cities. It was formerly a subsidiary of Air India and continues to provide crucial connectivity across smaller destinations.

Shankh Air

Shankh Air is a new entrant into India’s aviation market, focusing on spiritual tourism and eco-friendly air travel. It aims to connect pilgrimage cities with sustainable aviation initiatives.
