IndiGo
IndiGo is India's largest airline by passengers and fleet size, operating over 2,200 daily flights to 128 destinations (91 domestic, 37 international) as of April 2025. Headquartered in Gurgaon, it is known for affordable, on-time, and hassle-free operations as a low-cost carrier, offering only economy class seating and buy-on-board meals.

Air India Express
Air India Express, a Tata subsidiary of Air India, operates 500+ daily flights connecting 38 domestic and 17 international airports with a fleet of 110 aircraft. It offers personalized travel experiences, hot meals, comfortable seating, and exclusive loyalty benefits, all supported by a strong digital platform.

Alliance Air
Alliance Air is a regional airline in India, operating as a subsidiary of AI Assets Holding Ltd. It connects smaller cities and remote regions with major hubs, focusing on affordable and reliable regional connectivity. Its fleet primarily consists of ATR turboprop aircraft.
